## Scope

This mapping includes:

### Federal Institutions  
- **House of Commons** (representation layer)  
- **Senate** (review & safeguard layer)  
- **Prime Minister & Cabinet** (execution layer)  
- **Governor General** (constitutional layer)  
- **Elections Canada** (identity & access layer)  

### Legal & Oversight Framework  
- **The Charter of Rights and Freedoms**  
- **Supreme Court & federal courts**  
- **Officer of Parliament institutions** (Auditor General, Privacy Commissioner, etc.)

### Federal Operational Domains  
- Criminal law  
- Defence & immigration  
- Taxation & national programs  
- National registries & digital identity opportunities
